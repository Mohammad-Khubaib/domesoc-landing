How it works

Three networks.
One AI brain.

DomeSOC's architecture is designed from first principles — not retrofitted from a legacy SIEM. Each network handles a distinct layer of the threat lifecycle.

🔮
Predictive Network
Behavioral baselining and anomaly pre-scoring. Flags deviations before they become incidents — not just reacting to known signatures.
PREVENTION LAYER
🔍
Detective Network
Real-time MITRE ATT&CK mapping and confidence scoring. Collapses dozens of noisy alerts into a small number of prioritized threat decisions.
DETECTION LAYER
🧠
Advisor Network
Powered by Anthropic Claude. Shows its reasoning on every decision. Executes SOAR playbooks with analyst approval and a full audit trail.
RESPONSE LAYER
→  Human-in-the-loop by default  ·  Autonomous containment limited to Sev 4–5 with analyst pre-approval  ·  Every decision logged and explainable
