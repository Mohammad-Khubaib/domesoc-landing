Full autonomous mode

The AI acts.
You review.

Three operating modes — from human-in-the-loop to fully autonomous. You decide how much control the AI has. Every action is logged, explained, and reversible.

👁️

Supervised Mode

AI detects and recommends. Every action requires analyst approval. Full visibility, zero autonomous execution. Perfect for teams getting started.

⚡

Autonomous Mode

AI acts autonomously below your confidence threshold. High-confidence threats get contained immediately. Lower-confidence detections go to analyst queue.

🔴

Full Autonomous Mode

Zero human intervention. AI detects, decides, and executes containment on all threats. Designed for mature security teams. Requires legal acknowledgment. Every action logged.

📋

Set auto/approval/disabled independently for each SOAR action. Block IP automatically but require approval to isolate a host. Granular control over every response action.
